Health Check

Let Fujitsu identify and resolve potential issues with your Postgres database before they become a problem

A health check is preventive maintenance for your Postgres database. It is used to determine the likelihood of issues occurring with your Postgres database and infrastructure that may affect system performance, security and stability, and may adversely affect your business operations.

About the health check

We understand that every system is different and accordingly, we tailor the health check to your specific configuration and requirements.

A health check includes:

  • System diagnostics, identifying potential issues such as memory overflows, CPU and I/O bottlenecks, and network traffic and connection issues.
  • Evaluation of the operating system configuration. This includes I/O scheduling, memory and buffer/cache, security and network tuning.
  • Analysis of the Postgres setup. This includes postgresql.conf evaluation and tuning, feasibility evaluation for upgrading to the latest version of Postgres, audit of Postgres memory use and suitability of the existing hardware configuration.
  • Storage infrastructure recommendations. This determines whether storage configuration upgrades (such as using a different RAID level or purchasing SAN hardware) would enhance performance and provide a benefit for the client.

Health check outcome

We reveal areas of weakness and provide an action plan and best practice guidelines to ensure that your system is optimally configured and tuned, and is able to scale to meet future business needs.

And of course we can help you implement the recommendations laid out in the health check.

Bench Check

Let Fujitsu identify infrastructure bottlenecks and provide solutions

Fujitsu provides a benchmarking service to help determine if your database system is able to withstand stress and meet performance goals.

About database benchmarking

The purpose of database benchmarking is to measure the throughput and response times of database management systems, the results of which are compared against industry standards.

About the bench check

A bench check is a benchmarking program tailored by Fujitsu consultants to suit your specific configuration and workload. We capture real-time traffic and simulate database load under a variety of situations. We use transaction and CPU monitors to help provide the required information.

A bench check includes a review of the following:

  • Cloud configuration
  • Database configuration
  • Architectural design

Bench check outcome

Apart from the results of the benchmarking tests, we provide a report outlining potential issues identified during the benchmark tests, such as CPU and I/O bottlenecks. We provide solutions to help avoid and resolve those issues, and best practice guidelines to help streamline your operations.
