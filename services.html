Fujitsu is a leading global provider of IT services, and has the experience and resources needed to provide effective solutions for Postgres users and organizations looking for an open source alternative to their current database.

We offer the following services:

Database migration

Fujitsu can show how your organization can leverage one of the most widely used, mission-critical databases available, and reduce its database costs significantly without compromising quality, reliability or performance.

Health check

Fujitsu can identify and help resolve potential issues with your Postgres database before they become a problem.

Bench check

Fujitsu can test the performance of your Postgres database to determine how it compares against industry standards. We can help resolve the issues identified during the benchmark tests.

Operations review

Fujitsu can help streamline your organization's operations through best practice methodologies.

Consulting

Fujitsu provides professional consulting services for all your Postgres needs ‐ from rollout planning, installation, configuration and tuning, to strategic planning, problem‐solving, and more.
