Fujitsu and the PostgreSQL open source community

Fujitsu has been an active participant in open source development for over 35 years. As a long-term contributor to the development of open source database systems, Fujitsu is proud of its commitment to the promotion of PostgreSQL as a world-class enterprise database.

Fujitsu has been supporting PostgreSQL and the PostgreSQL open source community in various ways since 2004.

We have provided our ongoing support through various engagements with the PostgreSQL development community, including platinum sponsorship of the PGCon annual event, and through our development of new PostgreSQL features.

At Fujitsu, we freely share our know-how with the PostgreSQL community, and by opening up our knowledge base we can ensure that PostgreSQL is further strengthened, and continues to advance with Fujitsu as a partner.

Fujitsu's commitment to the PostgreSQL open source community:

  • Supported the PostgreSQL open source community since 2004
  • PGCon 2016 Platinum Sponsor
  • Provides advice to enterprises using PostgreSQL
  • Involved in development as well as advocacy
  • Contributed a number of enterprise features to PostgreSQL
