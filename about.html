About Fujitsu

Fujitsu is the leading Japanese ICT company and the 4th largest ICT service provider in the world, offering a full range of technology products, solutions and services. Established in 1935, Fujitsu is the second oldest IT company after IBM.

Around 160,000 Fujitsu employees support customers in over 100 countries.

Fujitsu has consolidated revenues of over US$45 billion.

Fujitsu builds and operates IT systems and services for clients in both the public and private sectors.

From telecommunications to healthcare, Fujitsu offer IT solutions to improve efficiency, increase productivity and reduce costs.
